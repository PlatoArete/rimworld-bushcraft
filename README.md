# RimWorld Bushcraft

RimWorld Bushcraft is a small RimWorld mod concept for land-based foraging inspired by fishing zones.

Players designate a Bushcraft Zone and assign pawns to a dedicated Bushcraft work type. Pawns then forage, track, trap, and gather modest survival yields from the local biome, with results affected by skills, season, weather, time of day, zone depletion, and eventually xenotype food preferences.

## Planned Features

- Dedicated `Bushcraft` work type.
- Land-based `Bushcraft Zone`.
- Biome-aware vanilla loot tables.
- Skill weighting from both `Plants` and `Animals`.
- Seasonal output changes, with reduced winter yields.
- Per-zone forage density and regeneration to prevent infinite free resources.
- Vanilla-first resources such as berries, herbal medicine, insect jelly, raw fungus, and insect meat.
- Future compatibility hooks for modded food, medicine, fungus, insect, and survival resources.

## Balance Target

Bushcraft is intended to be supplemental survival work rather than a direct replacement for farming, hunting, or ranching.

In high-yield conditions, one skilled pawn working full time should be able to feed roughly three colonists. In average or poor conditions, output should be lower and less reliable.
